BC 30,000 BC

As a term project in FIN 100 Art History, students were asked to analyze and critique a work of art from a period of histrory within the textbook and then come up with their own creative response in whatever form. I chose the artwork seen in prehistoric cave drawings found in France that are estimated to be well over 30,000 years old. My creative response was in two parts: I created a large drawing in the style and method of the ancient artists and produced a video documenting the entire project. This involved research, voice-over recordings, and even composing and recording the soundtrack.
